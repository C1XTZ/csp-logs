# Changelog

## New features, options and improvements

*   Roads fixed;
*   Dynamic ambient is back, plus its data is used for blurry reflections;
*   New shaders with heating volumes (check out exhaust pipes on GT3 cars);
*   Additional includes sections, variables and multiple sections at once for INI-files (sorry, I'll prepare parsers for other languages, but it's much easier to use now);
*   New parameters for sound events;
*   `EMISSIVE_ENGINE_TEMP` item for linking emissives to engine temperature;
*   `TOGGLE_VISIBILITY` and `TOGGLE_VISIBILITY_INVERSE` for `EMISSIVE_…`;
*   `BLINKING_PATTERN` and `BLINKING_DURATION` options for `EMISSIVE_…` and lights;
*    Fixes.
