# Changelog

## New features, options and improvements

*   New smoke (WIP, sometimes slow and not working online or in VR for now);
*   Extended physics:
    *   Negative offset for turbos;
    *   LUT for gas pedal for turbos;
*   Reflections are fixed;
*   Dry track reflections are fixed;
*   Freer camera:
    *   Option to only move in horizontal plane;
    *   Now compatible with accumulation blur shots, allowing to keep car sharper as long as you move camera accordingly before taking a shot;
*   Weather FX: dimmer lighting with clouds covering the sun (not quite cloud shadows yet);
*   Stability improved.
